Hi, I'm Elena, a 16 year old from Italy. I'm curious about many things, maybe too many and sometimes I burn out.
I'm learning cybersecurity, web development and game development. Not at the same time, of course. Some months I'm focused on one thing, other months I'm focused on something else

I love 2D pixel art games and 3D stylized games but playing them can be a little painful right now, because I know I can't recreate even the simplest ones yet, but they're also what motivates me to keep learning.
I was always fascinated by those hacking videos that showed a computer with a green text "Loading..." and then "Hacked". 10 year old me would probably be disappointed to know that it doesn't work like this. I'm still fascinated though

I hope I'll make original websites. They don't have to be "better" than others just "mine". This website isn't amazing yet, but I stopped waiting for the perfect moment and started building something anyways

This website will change whenever I learn something new or just want to experiment so don't be surprised if I just switch theme, add somethings new, or delete something else
