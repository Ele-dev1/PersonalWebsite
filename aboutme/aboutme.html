I'm learning cybersecurity, web development and game development. Not at the same time, of course. Some months I'm focused on one thing, other months I'm focused on something else

I love 2D pixel art games and 3D stylized games but playing them, at the moment, hurts a bit because I know I can't recreate the easiest of them.
I was always fascinated by those hacking videos that showed a computer with a green text "Loading..." and then "Hacked". 10 year old me would probably be disappointed to know that it doesn't work like this

I would also love to learn things like AI or robotics, not for a deep reason, they just look cool tbh

I hope I'll make original websites. They don't have to be "better" than others just "mine". At the moment, I'm writing this and I know that this webpage is not amazing yet, but at least I stopped overthinking, waiting for the perfect moment and started making something

This website will change whenever I learn something new or just feel like it so don't be surprised if I just switch theme, add somethings new, or delete something else
